About Me

3D Modeler | 3D Visualiser | CAD Specialist

I enjoy bringing designs to life by creating realistic, high-impact visual content using tools like AutoCAD, Blender and Twinmotion. I collaborate closely with my clients to understand their goals and turn ideas into compelling visual outputs that highlight the essence of each project.

Whether working with technical drawings, or building 3D scenes from the ground up without precise requirements, I combine accuracy with creative vision to produce detailed models that feel right. From high-resolution images to immersive video walkthroughs, I focus on delivering polished media that provides clear design communication and presentation.

I also enjoy crafting natural environments including landscapes and vegetation, as well as man-made features such as roads, paths and artificial lighting adding authenticity and atmosphere to every scene. My work blends precision with imagination to help clients showcase their ideas in the most engaging and visually compelling way.

Supplementary Services

In addition to the above, I can offer a range of complementary services:

I am experienced in BIM modeling, using tools like Revit and Navisworks to produce accurate, data-rich building models that support design coordination and asset management.
